Terms and Conditions
To participate in the ED3 Home Performance with ENERGY STAR Program, the following conditions apply:

    •  The homeowner must be a current ED3 residential customer and reside in a single-family detached home.

    •  Program, procedures, and requirements are subject to change or cancellation without notice.

    •  Contracting company performing Home Energy Audit must be on the Participating Contractors list for the Arizona Home Performance with ENERGY STAR® program located on the ED3 website.

    •  One Home Performance with ENERGY STAR® Energy Audit is allowed per location.
