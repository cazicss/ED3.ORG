Employment Opportunities







Electrical District No. 3 (ED3) is a public power utility that was formed in 1926. ED3 distributes power to the city of Maricopa, the town of Stanfield as well as the agricultural community in the surrounding area. The recent growth within our service area has created opportunities for various positions within our organization. ED3 offers an excellent benefits package and has many other options to offer its employees.
