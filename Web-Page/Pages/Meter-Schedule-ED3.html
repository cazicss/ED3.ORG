Electrical District No. 3 Meter Read Dates


Current Meter Schedule

Please click the above link that shows as "Current Meter Schedule". Match the billing cycle that is shown on your billing statement to the cycle that is indicated to the far left. Each month of the year is listed on the top of the schedule, just follow along from left to right to determine which day your meter will be read by the District.

Note: Keep in mind that these dates are not permanent and could change from 3-5 days. Please call 520-424-9021 with any questions or concerns. Thank you.
